Rice with strawberries

Ingredients
25 g of butter
1 liter of milk
100 g short grain round rice
1/2 cup of black olives
a small pinch of salt
1 tablespoon of vanilla sugar
2 tablespoons of sugar
approx. 300 g of strawberries + 2 tablespoons of sugar

Preparation

➞ In a wide pot, melt the butter. Add milk, rice and salt.
➞ Bring to a boil, reduce the heat and cook uncovered over low heat for about 40 minutes, stirring frequently with a wooden spoon.
➞ At the very end, add the vanilla sugar and sugar and stir until dissolved.
➞ Wash the strawberries, cut off the stalks, cut them into pieces, then mash them with a fork with added sugar.
➞ Place the rice in bowls and pour over the strawberry sauce.
