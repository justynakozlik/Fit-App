Pasta with chicken, zucchini, feta cheese and sun dried tomatoes

Ingredients
160 g of pasta
1 single chicken fillet
2 cloves of garlic
1 teaspoon of dried oregano
2 tablespoons of extra virgin olive oil
1 small zucchini (about 250 g)
5 pieces of dried tomatoes in olive oil
100 g of feta cheese
optional: grated parmesan / grana padano / other long-maturing cheese

Preparation

➞ Set salted water to cook the pasta.
➞ Dice the chicken fillet and season with salt, pepper, grated garlic, and oregano. Then mix it with 1 tablespoon of olive oil (you can leave it to marinate for a few hours if you have time).
➞ Put the pasta on the boiling water and cook until al dente.
➞ Heat a larger frying pan, pour in the remaining tablespoon of olive oil and put in the chicken. Fry for 1 - 2 minutes while stirring.
➞ Add the zucchini, cut into small cubes. Fry, stirring every now and then, for about 2 - 3 minutes, until the zucchini is tender.
➞ Finally, add the chopped sun-dried tomatoes and 2 tablespoons of olive oil from the jar. Season with freshly ground pepper.
➞ Put the drained pasta into the pan and stir it all the time, heating it up.
➞ Add diced feta cheese and mix. Heat up for a while and put away from heat. Can be served with Parmesan cheese.
