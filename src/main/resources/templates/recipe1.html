Marinated tofu

Ingredients
a cube of natural tofu
spoon of olive oil
salt
pepper
turmeric
smoked paprika
a teaspoon of maple syrup
a piece of ginger
clove of garlic
the juice of half a lemon

Preparation

➞ Grind the ginger on small eyes and squeeze the garlic through the press.
➞ Add ginger, garlic, olive oil, salt, pepper, turmeric, smoked paprika, maple syrup and the juice of half a lemon to the bowl. We mix thoroughly.
➞ Add sliced tofu to the resulting marinade. We marinate them for at least an hour in a cool place.
➞ Heat up the pan and add the tofu with the marinade.
➞ Stir gently and fry until the tofu turns golden. Serve with steamed vegetables and lime wedges.
